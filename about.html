Who is The Pixel Polygon?

Hi! I'm The Pixel Polygon, or just call me Matt. I've been playing video games from a very young age. Around ten years ago, I developed an interest in retro games, and always wanted to collect and play them. Right now, I maintain a collection with many games spanning from the mid 80's all the way to the present day. The Pixel Polygon is a channel for me to share my interest in gaming with the world.
